**Population Mean**

$\mu = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{N}{x_i}}{N}$

**Sample Mean**

$\bar{x} = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{N}{x_i}}{n}$

**Properties of Mean**

$\sum(x_i-\bar{x})=0$

**Measure of Dispersion** (bell-curve)

1. Range

2. Standard Deviations

3. Variance

Sample Variance

$s^2=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{N}{(x_i-\mu)^2}}{n-1}$

Population Variance

$\sigma^2=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{N}{(x_i-\mu)^2}}{N}$

- shows reliability
